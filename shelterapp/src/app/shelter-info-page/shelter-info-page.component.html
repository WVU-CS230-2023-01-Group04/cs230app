Park County Animal Shelter (PCAS) is a registered Wyoming nonprofit with humble beginnings that stretch back to August of 1990.

Since 1996, we have been serving the greater Cody and Park County areas by providing a safe, no-kill shelter for all abandoned, stray, abused, neglected, and relinquished animals. In addition to our shelter services, we also offer low-cost microchips and vaccines, and spay/neuter assistance through our SNIP Voucher Program.

We are truly dedicated to the preservation of life and quality existence for all companion animals. Join us in our mission.
